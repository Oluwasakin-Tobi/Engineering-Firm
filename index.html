The Company

Offshore wind Nigeria Limited is a leading Engineering, Onshore and Offshore, Fabrication, Construction and Procurement Company servicing the Oil & Gas Industry. Since our incorporation in January, 2014; we have invested in manpower and technological development hence our ability to offer tailor-made and cost effective solutions to whatever challenge our clients may have.

Ensuring client satisfaction as the end result, Offshore Wind is committed to providing innovative and cost effective solutions to meet your needs at all time, Quality is never compromised in our operations. Our operations has grown through technical leadership in the industry, provision of highly customized and cost effective services and through dedicated partnerships with our clients.

With our responsive and highly professional project management teams, we are able to deliver to our clients total capacity in services, ranging from preliminary studies to engineering designs and planning through materials, procurement, fabrication and installation to startup commissioning. This is evidenced in our completed and on-going projects.

At Offshore Wind, we want to exceed the expectations of our clients while providing safe, world-class and cost effective solutions in oil and gas engineering and construction services. We always want to exemplify employee pride while increasing confidence and ensuring great returns for all stake holders, We try always to inculcate the following principles in all facets of our operations.

Teamwork: OFFSHORE WIND depends greatly on effective teamwork between the employ for the successes achieved thus far. This value-enhancing team Work now pervades all activities at Offshore Wind including our relationships, our clients, suppliers and partners. We operate on the basis of a shared vision which is characterized by respect, openness, and trust of responsibility.

Integrity: At Offshore Wind, we are committed to high ethical standards and sound business integrity in all our operations. This have helped in establishing effective business relationship and trust between offshore wind and her clients.

FACILITIES AND EQUIPMENTS

The operational base of OFFSHORE WIND is located at Bonny Island, Rivers State. This facility has a fabrication and engineering work shop, blasting and paint workshop, electrical/ mechanical workshop, warehouse and open material storage facilities. Offshore Wind has partnered with two companies that owns jetty - making load out to barges and berthing of vessels whenever the need arises. We are well equipped to execute our project.
